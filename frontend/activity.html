Co-Curricular Activities

At PMMMCE, we believe in the harmonious development of the body, mind, and spirit. We organize various activities throughout the year.

Cultural & Literary Activities

The college organizes debates, essay writing compositions, music competitions, and drama to encourage self-expression and cultural appreciation among students.

Annual Day celebrations are the highlight of the academic year, showcasing student talent.

Sports & Games

Annual Sports Meet is organized to promote physical fitness and team spirit. Facilities for cricket, volleyball, badminton, and kho-kho are available.

Community Service

Students actively participate in community service programs, awareness rallies, and environmental protection campaigns (Tree Plantation) to develop social responsibility.
