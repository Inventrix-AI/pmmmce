Courses Offered

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility: Graduation / Post Graduation with minimum 50% marks.

Intake: 100 Seats (2 Units)

The B.Ed program is designed to prepare effective secondary school teachers. The curriculum includes theoretical knowledge, pedagogical skills, and practical training (Internship).

Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed)

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks.

Intake: 50 Seats

The D.El.Ed program aims to prepare teachers for the elementary stage of education (Class I to VIII).

Fee Structure

The fee structure is as per the norms of the State Government and the Affiliating University. Please contact the college office for the detailed fee structure for the current academic session.
