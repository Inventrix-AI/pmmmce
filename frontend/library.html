The College Library

The library is the heart of our institution, providing a rich collection of resources to support teaching, learning, and research. It creates a welcoming environment for students to engage in self-study.

Key Features

  • Collection: Over 5,000 books covering Education, Psychology, Science, and Literature.
  • Journals: Subscription to 10+ National and International journals.
  • Digital Access: Computers with internet access for research.
  • Reading Room: A quiet and spacious area with a seating capacity of 50 students.
