Admission Guidelines

Admissions to B.Ed and D.El.Ed courses are done through the Pre-B.Ed and Pre-D.El.Ed entrance examinations conducted by CG Vyapam, followed by online counseling by SCERT, Raipur.

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Marksheets
  • Graduation Marksheets (All Years/Semesters)
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Migration Certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport Size Photos
