Resource Centers & Laboratories

Practical training is a key component of teacher education. We have well-equipped labs to facilitate hands-on learning.

Science Laboratory

Equipped with necessary apparatus and chemicals for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology experiments, enabling student-teachers to demonstrate scientific concepts effectively.

ICT / Computer Resource Center

A modern computer lab with internet connectivity to train students in the use of technology in education (ICT). Includes projectors and audio-visual aids.

Psychology Resource Center

Contains various standardized psychological tests (IQ, Personality, Aptitude) and apparatus to help students understand learner psychology.

Arts & Crafts Resource Center

A creative space for students to learn making teaching aids, models, charts, and engage in workshops.
